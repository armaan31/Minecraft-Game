MINECRAFT

Block Width = 30 pixels

Block Height = 30 pixels

To control the player, use the arrow keys. Press Shift+p to make a block larger. Press Shift+m to make the block smaller. To make a block, click on of the following keys: w=wall block. g=ground block. r=roof block. t=trunk block. d=dark green block. l=light green block. c=cloud block. y=yellow wall block. u=unique block.
